## Flight Delay Analysis Dashboard (Tableau)

---

## 1. Dashboard Goals
- Help airport stakeholders understand delay patterns  
- Compare airline and route performance  
- Visualize operational bottlenecks  
- Identify peak delay periods  

---

## 2. Planned Visualizations

### 2.1 Delay Heatmap
- X: Hour of day  
- Y: Month  
- Color: Avg delay  

### 2.2 Airline Performance Comparison
- Bar chart: average delay per airline  
- Bar chart: on-time percentage  
- Optional: cancellation rate  

### 2.3 Route-Level Performance
- Map or bar: routes with highest delays  

### 2.4 Seasonal Delay Trends
- Line chart: average delay across the year  

### 2.5 Traffic vs Delay Scatter
- X: flights per day  
- Y: avg delay  

---

## 3. Filters
- Airline  
- Route  
- Month  
- Day of week  

---

## 4. Dashboard Layout
- Clean, minimalistic
- 1-page dashboard  
- KPIs at the top (OTP, avg delay, cancellation rate)
